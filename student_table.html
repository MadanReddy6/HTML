“I am a student” is the correct way to say your sentence. All singular nouns in English must have some sort of a determiner, (a, the, my etc.) A proper noun does not use a determiner—I am Julia. If you say, “*I am student” you sound like some strange kind of superhero.“I am a student” is the correct way to say your sentence.
